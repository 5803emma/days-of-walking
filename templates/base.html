Welcome to 100 Days of Walking!

Take up the challenge of walking for just thirty minutes a day for 100 days. It's a simple idea. Just walk as fast or as slow as you like, for at least thirty minutes a day, for 100 days. Share your favourite walking route, or have a look at some walks to get inspired ! Everyone can do it - old, young, fit, unfit, all shapes and sizes. It's good for the body and the mind.
